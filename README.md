## 👋 About Me

I'm a 6th-semester **BS Bioinformatics student at NUST SINES**, working at the intersection of
embedded systems, machine learning, and computational biology. My focus is building
**affordable, deployable healthcare technology** for low-resource settings in Pakistan —
from AI malaria diagnostics to EMG-driven rehabilitation robotics.

- 🏥 **MalariaSense** — AI malaria detection device, FICS International Stage 3 Finalist
- 📰 **IEEE ICIT 2026** — Paper accepted on AI-powered facial recognition attendance systems
- 🤖 **NeuroRevive** — Final Year Project: EMG soft robotic glove for post-stroke rehabilitation
- 💻 **Booted Linux on RISC-V** — Custom LiteX/VexRiscv SoC on Nexys A7-100T FPGA
- 🌍 **ISR @ SCALE, NUST** — Institutional Student Representative
- 📍 Karachi / Islamabad, Pakistan
