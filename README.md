## 👋 About Me

I'm a 6th-semester BS Bioinformatics student at **NUST SINES**, Islamabad, building affordable AI-powered medical devices for underserved populations in Pakistan. My work sits at the intersection of **embedded systems**, **machine learning**, and **computational biology**.

- 🔬 **Research focus:** Medical imaging AI, soft robotics for rehabilitation, computational neuroscience
- 🏥 **Mission:** Make diagnostic and rehabilitation technology accessible at scale in low-resource settings
- 📰 **IEEE ICIT 2026** — Paper accepted on AI-powered facial recognition attendance systems
- 🏆 **FICS International** — Stage 3 Finalist with MalariaSense
- 🌍 **ISR @ SCALE, NUST** — Institutional Student Representative
- 📍 Based in **Karachi / Islamabad, Pakistan**
