### 👋 Executive Summary

I am an undergraduate **Bioinformatics** researcher at the **National University of Sciences and Technology (NUST SINES)**, specializing at the intersection of embedded systems, machine learning, and computational biology. My primary focus is on engineering high-impact, cost-effective diagnostic and neurorehabilitation technologies tailored for resource-constrained healthcare environments.

  - 🏥 **MalariaSense:** Stage 3 Finalist at the FICS International Competition.
  - 📰 **Research Publication:** Co-authored and accepted paper on AI-driven facial recognition at **IEEE ICIT 2026**.
  - 🤖 **NeuroRevive (Senior Project):** Developing an 8-channel EMG soft robotic glove for post-stroke rehabilitation.
  - 💻 **Computer Architecture:** Successfully synthesized and booted Linux on a custom RISC-V SoC (LiteX/VexRiscv) utilizing a Nexys A7-100T FPGA.
  - 🌍 **Leadership:** Institutional Student Representative (ISR) at SCALE, NUST.
  - 📍 **Location:** Islamabad / Karachi, Pakistan.
